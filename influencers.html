The Factions

MEET

The Influencers

Influencer cards represent people you control that have influence over others in society. You send them out to sway people to your side. Each faction has 7 influencer cards. Influencers are used to grant bonuses to a storyline. Their bonuses range from 1 through 3 with the ability to level up with experience points (XP). Each influencer is associated with one of the twelve realms of influence.

The Equity Front

Haley S. Novitski

Social Worker

Haley's parents were both miners who came to Ceres for the chance at a better life. They were some of the first colonists to settle at Nueva Palenque. As the settlement grew up, new social challenges arose. Haley formed the Diana Network, named after the Roman goddess of crossroads. The network provides counseling, food and housing assistance, healthcare, job training and foster care services.

Haley is well respected among her peers and carries considerable clout among the people of Nueva Plaenque and the neighboring areas.

Base Value: 3 XP: 0 Streets

MacKenzie Patel

Labor Agency Owner

When MacKenzie first came to Ceres, he worked many odd jobs to support himself as a new settler and saw an inherent flaw in the system. Years later, he identified a successful business model creating temporary jobs for short-term hauls. His company, the Phoenixwalk, ensures workers are paid fairly and have the necessary support between jobs.

MacKenzie is seen as a revolutionary in the labor market and often praised for his fair, equitable treatment of all workers over profits.

Base Value: 2 XP: 3 Labor

Xavier Marone

Political Lobbyist

Xavier came to Ceres with little more than the clothes on his back. He was quickly welcomed into one of the open communes of Ceres where anyone willing to exchange services or goods could find a home. He later founded Fresh Start, a political lobbying group that promotes egalitarism, hoping everyone on Ceres can find similiar acceptance.

Xavier is incredibly patient and non-confrontational, which is very helpful in conveying Fresh Start's ideals.

Base Value: 2 XP: 2 Politics

Shifty Charlie

Punk Singer

Charlie visited Ceres many times on tour before deciding to settle there. She is a founding member of the post-Terran punk band, Mass Solstice. Her lyrics have a considerable effect on influencing fans. She has used her fame to help fight against hunger, believing that any government spending on war and defense while their people starve is inherently wrong. Mass Solstice fills their concerts with people from all walks of life, which effectively spreads their message.

Shifty Charlie is the founder of post-Terran political punk on Ceres. Thousands of fans look to her for guidance.

Base Value: 2 XP: 1 Entertainment

Jane Nakagawa

Docudrama Producer

Jane Nakagawa rose to fame when she won at Solswing, Ceres' independent film festival, for her docudrama about inequities in the educational system. Jane has the investigative talent of a journalist and the storytelling skills of an artist. One of Ceres' streaming services has given her a 24-episode series which has risen to the top of the charts.

While Jane can sometimes be seen as a snoop, her personality makes her seem unintrusive and trusting.

Base Value: 1 XP: 3 Media

Morgan Terzic

Mystic Leader

The Hermetic Order of Itokawa believes we find wisdom through the study of the earliest objects of our solar system, such as the asteroids. Morgan was raised within the Order and at a very early age, they identified him as destined to lead the group. As humans and celestial objects all come from the same material, Morgan espouses the belief that humans should treat all things as equal and in harmony. This belief has gained popularity as more of the solar system is explored.

The people of Ceres revere Morgan as a being wise beyond their years. Even those not in the order, look to them kindly and with respect.

Base Value: 2 XP: 1 Occult

Kala Macdonald

Crime Family Lieutenant

Kala had always dreamed of being a schoolteacher. Then an unfortunate case of gambling debt got her mixed up with the Falcon crime family. She found she was actually much better at keeping criminals in line than children. Kala worked off her debt, running small repo jobs, and eventually worked her way up to lieutenant. The Falcons are a prestigious family in the gambling business and run the best casinos in the Belt.

Kala is intimidating and can usually get her way with a just a single look.

Base Value: 1 XP: 1 Underworld

Galactic Artists, Inc.

Li Xquenda Megalos

Fashionista

Li was very popular on Venus, where she started her clothing line. However, their general xenophobia and isolationism made it difficult to expand. Li exploded onto the Belt scene with a carefully planned media campaign and a good deal of her found wealth. Ceres ate up the new and foreign styles that Li offered. Her designs have become so popular that her style is ubiquitous in everyday Ceres life.

Li is quiet by design. She gets everyone to hang on her every word by using so few.

Base Value: 3 XP: 0 Entertainment

Alastair

Psychic Medium

In a transport shuttle accident, Alastair was left without oxygen for over three minutes. After they revived him, Alastair saw and heard spirits of the dead. For over a decade now, Alastair has used the spirits to make predictions on his holo-vid show. His successful prediction of the invention of Blergmar and the rise to power of Queen Artemis has launched Alastair into famedom and made him one of the network's most famous personalities.

Alastair is a showman and uses his dark, brooding demeanor to his advantage.

Base Value: 2 XP: 3 Occult

Lola Chizimu

Hollywood Financier

Lola Chizimu grew up in the hills of Hollywood, USA, Earth. Her bright intellect and incredible ability with numbers made her a significant fortune as a business manager to the stars. When approached for financing projects, she found that her gut was often right on whether to contribute. It became a game for her, and one that she was very good at. Eventually, Lola became bored with the same films and decided to try her hand on Ceres, where everything was new and exciting.

Lola is fun to be around and often the life of the party.

Base Value: 2 XP: 2 Finance

Wu Bai

Celebrity Engineer

Wu Bai won their first engineering contest at age six with an AI-powered friend-bot. They became the youngest battle bot arena champion on Ceres. The Creepy Crawler, the Moonraker, and the Dust Derby are all champion bots invented and built by Bai. Everyone wants to be Bai's friend in hopes some of their luck and smarts will rub off. Bai loves their newfound celebrity status and never misses a chance to be interviewed.

Bai is energetic and insightful, but can sometimes ramble too much about technical details.

Base Value: 2 XP: 1 Elite

Hector de la Costa

Union Rep

Hector found his first job on Ceres as a Flare Star Pictures apprentice. He was often getting into trouble, but managed to make his way up to a journeyman. Hector had many impressionable young apprentices under his control. Some called it a clique and others called it a gang. They were known for solving studio problems while using less than moral or legal methods, but never seemed to get caught. Hector now represents studio employees in the Moving Pictures Tech Workers Union.

Hector is a fast-talker who can work his way out of any trouble. He can be very convincing.

Base Value: 1 XP: 3 Labor

Kitty Juventas Aue

Ambassador

Kitty was a child star known for her role in the long run series, The First Miners of Ceres. She starred in a few well-known holovids such as, Galactic Story, The Messier Effect, and Return to Earth. She retired early from her acting career to focus on humanitarian efforts between Earth and Mars. For many years, she has served as the Ambassador for Mars on Ceres. She has found a home among New Hollywoodland and has settled nicely on the protoplanet.

Everyone she meets adores Kitty. She is patient, soft-spoken and a great listener.

Base Value: 1 XP: 2 Politics

Willie Jansen

Professional Tipster

Willie Jansen is Ceres' native son. His moms ran Ma's Chili Bowl, a staple of New Hollywoodland. Willie ran around the aisles of the restaurant from an early age, eventually working there as a teen, and taking over when his moms retired. All the big stars find themselves at Ma's after a long night of partying. Willie is always available with a hot cup of coffee, a friendly ear, and words of advice. What they don't know is that the business has been suffering for years and it only stays afloat by his careful curation and sale of hot celebrity gossip.

Willie is boisterous and welcoming. His warm affection creates a trustworthy environment. He is very good at hiding his secret.

Base Value: 1 XP: 1 Media

Ceres Institute of Technology

Edita Erna Kemp

Defense Contractor

Kemp's family includes a long line of military service on Mars, though her father retired to Ceres when she was still a little girl. As an adult, Edita used her strategic mind and family contacts to work her way up through the military industrial complex. As head of Sol Corp, Edita has secured the single largest defense contract with the Ceres Consortium and is responsible for nearly 60% of the personnel who protect the Main Asteroid belt. She hires top scientists to ensure their weapons technology are the best in the system.

Edita is direct and to the point. Her disciplined demeanor can be off-putting, but she can be trusted to tell the truth.

Base Value: 3 XP: 0 Research

Robin M. Prinyanka

Wealthy Inventor

Robin Prinyanka's generational wealth has assured that they were afforded all the luxuries offered in life. They went to all the best private prep schools, was accepted into historic Earth colleges, and had the startup funds to start their own business at the age of 21. Robin decided to go to Ceres where regulations were weak, laws were lenient, and the intellectuals seen as elite. Robin is a brilliant inventor and created the revolutionary solar drill rig, changing the trajectory of Ceres forever.

Robin is smug and insensitive. They know one word from them can make or break any inventor.

Base Value: 2 XP: 3 Elite

Stiger C. Kovaevi

Aribter

Stiger lived a long and prestigious life as a respected lawyer and judge on Earth. After some health issues, Stiger chose to live among the colony ships to take advantage of the Zero-G units. Colonists often find themselves stuck in the middle of disputes during their long trip between Earth and the Belt. Stiger acts as an arbiter, finding non-violent means to come to a fair agreement between all parties involved. Over the years, he has accumulated many favors and been left with more than a few disgruntled clients.

Colonists view Stiger as an unbiased third party. He is a careful, strategic man who often will only take cases he can win.

Base Value: 2 XP: 2 Law

Frances H. De With

Venture Capitalist

Frances has five older brothers. Each one is a scientist- an astronomer, a biologist, botanist, pharmacologist, and a mineralogist. He could never quite settle on a science that suited him, but was quite good in the tech sector. He founded Cred-tech through his innovative use of social networks, crowdfunding, and cryptocurrency. It is now the number one venture capitalist firm for STEM-related business. His brothers, as the only board members, provide sound advice.

Frances is upbeat and fearless. He makes everything life gives him a fun challenge.

Base Value: 2 XP: 1 Finance

Raharjo Enok

Space Dockers CTO

Enok was always good with technology, especially with mechanics and automation. His first job out of school was as a shuttle mechanic until his boss realized his talents were not being fully used. They put him in charge of automating the original Ceres space dock. Enok worked aside engineers, AI programmers, mechanics and others to build the first fully AI-driven space dock. He helped create, Space Dockers Inc., the premiere provider of automated space docks in the asteroid belt.

People love to work with Enok because he is always upbeat and positive. This is a good thing, since he lives to work

Base Value: 1 XP: 3 Transportation

Citra Afolayan

Sci Tech Sisterhood Leader

Citras parents were killed when political conflict turned deadly on Earth. They sent her and thousands of orphans of conflict to Ceres as a method to ease overpopulation. Aboard the colony ship, the Shashti, she learned to make friends among the ships youth. From a young age, she was a natural leader and found a way to bring people together. Once settled on Ceres, as a teenager, Citra couldnt find a place to belong. She ran away from her adoptive familys home and opted for a life on the streets. She fell in with the Sci-Tech Sisterhood and rose among the ranks to become the leader.

Citra is ruthless. She will do whatever is necessary to protect what she sees as hers.

Base Value: 1 XP: 2 Underworld

Kaiti Eliana Gabor

Street Hacker

Kaiti grew up on the Ceres Institute of Technology main campus. Her parents were both prestigeous, tenured professors of the insitution. As she entered her teen years, she grew frustrated with the elitist attitude of the students and employees. Kaiti, along with a group of fellow CIT dropouts, embarked on a mission to repurpose discarded technology found on campus. Their goal is to redistribute these resources to the less fortunate denizens of the streets of Ceres.

Kaiti is patient and kind. She believes everyone has a chance to rise above their station and does whatever she can to live by this tenant.

Base Value: 1 XP: 1 Streets
