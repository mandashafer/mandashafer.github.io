ABOUT

Game Overview

The Establishment is a GMless, card-based roleplaying game about a secret society controlling a space-faring humanity's future.

While it uses cards to keep track of the action, there are no points-based objectives for winning. Like most roleplaying games, your goal is to play out your faction and to achieve an end to the story you think best fits.

The game is played over four rounds. You choose a path you agree with on storyline cards. Then you argue, make deals, and support other players by playing influencers. A society card, representing the whims of society, will determine which way the story goes.

THE RULES

How to Play

Players will choose their faction and receive a deck of seven influencer cards. Draft for description cards that will help you roleplay your faction.

1 2 3 4 5

There are three storylines in each round. A single storyline card gives players information about the current situation and then two options they can vote on. Players place their influencers on the side they favor on any of the three storyline cards.

They describe how they send these influencers out to do their bidding. It may be a favored owed, a plea from a loved one, or even blackmail. The players decide and narrate this as part of their story.

1 2 3

Each storyline is resolved by adding up the value of each influencer, any experience points, and any matching realm icon bonuses. However, a society card which represents the whims of the people can drastically change the landscape. Specific realm icons are cancelled so none of those influencers bonuses will count. In addition, a bonus is added to one of the sides.

In the example below, before the society card, the score for the first option (left) would be 8 with three cards played (3+2+2) and one matching realm bonus (+1). The society card flips elite and finance influencers. The left side only has a value of 3 with a +1 bonus from the society card which loses to the right side with is a total of 5 with the three cards played (1+2+1), one XP star (+1) and no matching realm bonus.

1 2 3 4

Game End

The game ends after 4 rounds when all storylines have concluded with an epilogue. Players are encouraged to discuss how the epilogues affect their factions.

THE STORY SO FAR

Game Setting

After the successful colonization of Mars, climate crises and overpopulation on Earth lead to a mass migration of humans to other settlements.

Humans have established themselves on the inner planets (Venus, Earth and Mars), within the Main Asteroid Belt based on Ceres, and the outer planets (Jupiter and Saturn) and their moons. Ceres began as a mining town, but grew as miners and their families decided to stay and call Ceres home.

PLAY AS ONE

Five Factions to Choose

Play as the leader of one of five different factions found on Ceres. Each faction has different realms of influence, such as transportation, politics, or entertainment, that can be used to team up with other players.

Rabblerousers, Anarchs, Social Justice Warriors

The Equity Front

Concerned about justice and fairness, members of this group fight for everyone to have equal treatment.

Mobsters, Cabbies, Teamsters

The Taxi Union

An organized criminal enterprise masquerading as a legitimate union, they control how people and goods get around the Belt as well as the media.

Nerds, Scientists, STEMers

Ceres Institute of Technology

Representing the best and brightest on Ceres, this group is filled with doctors, lawyers, researchers and engineers from all the sciences.

Stars, Execs, Moneybags, Cultists

Galactic Artists Inc.

They embody classic Hollywood - the glitz, glamour and mystery. Rumors are their success is due to an embedded secret society.

Queens, Blue bloods, Royals

The Descendants

A group of well-off individuals with ancestry traced back to the kings and queens of Earth.

HOW THE WORLD WORKS

Realms of Influence

Economy

Finance

The banking and business sector of the world, it includes activities such as the lending of money, making purchases, betting on stocks, investing, and making money. Influencers in this realm include bankers, financial advisors, wealthy investors, and crypto currency experts.

Transportation

Moving of goods and people across as well as to and from Mars. It includes commercial or leisure travel, immigration, shipping, and importing/exporting. Influencers in this realm include dock workers, ship's captains, logistics experts, truckers, or public transportation workers.

Labor

The working class and industry within the word, it includes activities surrounding mass production, factories, utilities, building projects, office workers, physical labor, service industry and AI or robotic work. Influencers in this realm include union representatives, owner of staffing agency, service workers and utility executives.

Culture

Research

Pursuits of the mind and science, including institutions such as universities, research laboratories, libraries, and history or science museums. Influencers in this realm include doctors, engineers, biologists, historians, and professors.

Entertainment

Events and activities that people do for fun including the arts, theater, music, movies, parties, television, and sports. Influencers in this realm include movie stars, top athletes, famous painters, and fashion designers.

Media

The spread of information between people in society. It includes such things as traditional news media, online networks, advertising, and mass communications. Influencers in this realm include news reporters, investigative journalists, official spokespersons, and popular talking heads.

Governance

Politics

The administration of government and policy-making groups. Influencers in this realm include elected leaders, appointed administrators, pollsters, and lobbyists.

Underworld

The seedy and corrupt underbelly of society, this includes the activities of organized crime, human traffickers, illegal gambling, smuggling, etc. Influencers in this realm include, mob bosses, grifters, white-collar criminals and their associates.

Law

The rules and regulations put onto society and the institutions that support them. This would include any peace forces, court systems, legislative bodies, security forces and military. Influencers in this realm include judges, lawyers, peace officers, and military.

Society

Street

The average person and the more common elements of society, including the lower- and middle- classes. This group also includes low-level criminals, street gangs, the homeless and disenfranchised. Influencers in this real include social workers, gang leaders, neighborhood watch leaders, and hackers.

Occult

Belief in the spiritual, mystical, or supernatural. This can range from cults to organized religion. It includes mysterious things that science cannot yet explain: UFO sightings, rumors of ghosts or vampires, psychic impressions, and the such. Influencers in this realm include pastors, shamans, ghost hunters, and psychics.

Elite

The upper class of people in society, this group includes those who have great power and wealth. Influencers in this realm include wealthy investors, privileged philanthropists, well-connected debutantes, and people with ‘old world’ money. 

About the Designer

Manda Shafer is a first time designer who, like many, found a lot of free time in 2020 to work on a game idea.

Manda is a storyteller, a designer, a play tester, a dungeon master, a maker, and now, a game developer. With the launch of The Establishment, she is bringing her love of crafting an engaging story and the development of gaming mechanics together in a new and interactive way.

When not improving the player experience in The Establishment, you will find Manda caring for foster animals, teaching board games to new players, exploring new crafting options in her workshop or relaxing with her favorite cat, Hobbes.
